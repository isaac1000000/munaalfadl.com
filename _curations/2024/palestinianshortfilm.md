Welcome to our Experimental and Documentary Palestinian Short Film Screening at Bar Zakh! Join us for a captivating evening filled with thought-provoking films that shed light on Palestinian resistance and experiences with occupation. Immerse yourself in a diverse selection of short films that challenge traditional storytelling methods. This is a fantastic opportunity to engage with Palestinian cinema and filmmakers. All proceeds are being donated to UNRWA.

Featuring:
Al Quds by Vladimir Tamari
Ambience by Wisam Al-Jafari
Your father was born 100 years old, and so was the Nakba
The Story of Milk and Honey by Bassma AlSharif
Maqloubeh
Moonscape, by Mona Benyamin
Introduction to the End of an Argument, by Jayce Salloum & Elia Suleiman
